In this MDOB test, Bob travels to Vietnam, riding from Hanoi to Ho Chi Min city (previously known as Saigon). Checking out along the way places like Hoi An, and also areas such as the tunnels and places left in the war. The bike chosen for the ride was a trusty Honda 250XL, see picture on the left. This was easily able to deal with the semi rough terrain. Not unsurprisingly Bob describes the weather as Hot Damn Hot. Fortunately a thirst quenching beer costs around 4p or 1000VND in the local currency Vietnamese Dong (No sniggering westerners!!) and a million dong is around 40 (UK pounds).
