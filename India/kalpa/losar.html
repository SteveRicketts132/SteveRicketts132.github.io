Delhi/ Motorbikes and Trains

Loasar

Just a stop on the way to Minali, but some interesting meat dumplings after the food in Kibber. The road took us over the Rohhang Pass at 4000m. A local herdsman tied his yak up to our motorcycles. Hotels mean sharing a bed, curious as a room only cost £6 a night.

Beer

Alcohol is frowned on in India and so is expensive. There appeared to be only brand, Kingfisher. However you can buy what purports to be Scotch Whiskey at £4 a bottle or £5 for the premium brand.
