Minali and roads

Minali

As Kibber was the low point then Manali was the high point. It is a former hippy town of 2 halves. Down below is the busy city, up the valley are the bars and night clubs. A very enjoyable night spent listening to a Israeli group playing Oasis. There is always a place your would like to visit again and this was it on this trip. Even fish and chips, the fish being brown trout from the local rivers.

Hotels

I am not sure India gets the international traveller or maybe its just not interested. The hotels varied in standard, but the bathrooms were consistently a health & safety nightmare. Electric cables hang from the ceiling and the shower was a large bucket and a smaller jug. It worked fine.
