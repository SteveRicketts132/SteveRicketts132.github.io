I have travelled extensively in Europe, so I thought I was ready to go further a field. My grand father had served in India before the First World War. My Uncle served on a ship based in India and my father in law appeared to ride motorcycles around India, serving with the army after the end of the Second World War. TV programmes – Top Gear for one- cemented the idea of going to India, as well as people at work’s stories of visiting relatives, in India. In the planning it appeared my son and nephew would accompany me, so what could go wrong? Well high siding and sliding down a grass cliff, hitting a bus amongst other mishaps. It took me 2 weeks to get my head around motorcycling in India, but then again my father in law told stories of crashing into a ditch! So I should have been warned.
